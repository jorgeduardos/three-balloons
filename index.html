Who are we ?

We are a team of professional puppeteers, artists, educators and mothers having more than a decade of experience in the children entertainment and education business. Our work is our passion. We take pride in our original shows and the happy memories we create with children and their families.

Three Balloons Entertainment offers Puppet Shows, Puppet Workshops, Marionette Shows and Kids Entertainment in general for children of all ages in both English and Spanish. We are the only mobile kids entertainment company offering programs for kids of all ages in Spanish in the Houston area. Our mission is to help kids develop collaboration, creativity, problem-solving, resilience and empathy skills that will help them succeed as adults in the 21st century
